DejaVu Fonts, version 2.37

The DejaVu fonts are a font family based on the Vera Fonts.

Used for WebAssembly platform.

The sources can be found in tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/wasm/DejaVuSans.ttf, and tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/wasm/DejaVuSansMono.ttf.
