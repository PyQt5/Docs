FreeBSD strtoll and strtoull, version 18b29f3fb8abee5d57ed8f4a44f806bec7e0eeff

strtoll() and strtoull() are functions for converting a string to (unsigned) long long integer.

Used in Qt Core.

The sources can be found in tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/freebsd/strtoll.c and tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/freebsd/strtoull.c.
