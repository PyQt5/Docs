forkfd

Used on most Unix platforms in Qt Core.

The sources can be found in tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/forkfd/forkfd.c, tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/forkfd/forkfd.h, and tqtc-qt5/qtbase/src/3rdparty/forkfd/forkfd_gcc.h.
