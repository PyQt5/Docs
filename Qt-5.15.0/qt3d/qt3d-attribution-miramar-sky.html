Miramar Skybox Textures

High res environment map (converted to webgl).

Used in the anaglyph-rendering example and autotests of Qt 3D.

The sources can be found in tqtc-qt5/qt3d/examples/qt3d/exampleresources/assets/cubemaps/miramar.
